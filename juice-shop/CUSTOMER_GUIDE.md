## Getting Started

## Account Management

### Creating an Account
1. Click **"Sign Up"** in the top-right navigation
2. Fill in the registration form:
   - **Name**: Your full name
   - **Email**: Valid email address
   - **Password**: Secure password (min 6 characters)
3. Click **"Sign Up"** button
4. You'll be automatically logged in and redirected to the homepage

### Logging In
1. Click **"Login"** in the navigation bar
2. Enter your credentials:
   - Email
   - Password
3. Click **"Login"** button
4. Access granted! You're now logged in

### Guest Mode
- Browse all products without creating an account
- Add items to cart (temporary session)
- **Cannot checkout** - must create account first

## Cart & Checkout

### Adding Items to Cart
1. On product detail page, select **size**
2. Click **"Add to Cart"** button
3. Success notification appears
4. Cart icon updates with item count

### Managing Your Cart
1. Click **Cart icon** in navigation (shows item count)
2. Cart page displays:
   - All added items
   - Product image and name
   - Selected size
   - Quantity controls (+/-)
   - Price per item
   - Subtotal per item
   - **Total cart value**

#### Cart Actions
- **Increase/Decrease Quantity**: Use +/- buttons
- **Remove Item**: Click remove/delete button
- **Continue Shopping**: Return to browse more products
- **Proceed to Checkout**: When ready to order

### Checkout Process

#### Step 1: Review Cart
- Verify all items and quantities
- Check total amount
- Click **"Proceed to Checkout"**

#### Step 2: Delivery Information
Fill in the checkout form:
- **Customer Name**: Your full name
- **Email**: Contact email
- **Phone Number**: Mobile number
- **Delivery Address**: Complete address with:
  - Street/House number
  - Area/Locality
  - City
  - State
  - Pincode
- **Delivery Date**: Choose preferred delivery date

#### Step 3: Payment
- Currently supports **Cash on Delivery (COD)**
- Future payment methods coming soon

#### Step 4: Place Order
1. Review all details
2. Click **"Place Order"** button
3. Order confirmation appears
4. Redirect to **Order Confirmation** page

### Order Confirmation
After successful order:
- **Order ID** displayed
- **Order summary** with all items
- **Delivery details** confirmed
- **Total amount** shown
- **Estimated delivery date**
- Confirmation email sent (if configured)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Do I need to create an account to browse?
**A:** No, you can browse all products as a guest. However, you need an account to make purchases.

### Q: Can I modify my order after placing it?
**A:** Contact customer support immediately via chat. Orders can be modified if not yet processed.

### Q: How do I track my delivery?
**A:** Go to "My Orders" and click on your order to see current status.

### Q: What payment methods are accepted?
**A:** Currently Cash on Delivery (COD). More payment options coming soon.

### Q: Can I cancel an order?
**A:** Yes, contact support via chat. Pending orders can be cancelled.

### Q: How long does delivery take?
**A:** Delivery times vary by location. Choose your preferred date during checkout.

### Q: Are the juices fresh?
**A:** Yes! All juices are cold-pressed and delivered within 24 hours of preparation.

### Q: Can I save products for later?
**A:** Yes, use the Wishlist feature (heart icon) to save items.

### Q: How do I contact customer support?
**A:** Use the chat widget (bottom-right) or view chat history from your profile.

### Q: Is my cart saved if I logout?
**A:** Yes, your cart items are saved and will be there when you login again.
